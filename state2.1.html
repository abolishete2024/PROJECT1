Famous Temples in Maharashtra

Discover the spiritual richness of Maharashtra through its renowned temples.

Shirdi Sai Baba Temple

Located in Shirdi, this temple is dedicated to Sai Baba and attracts millions of devotees worldwide.

Trimbakeshwar Temple

This ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and located near Nashik.

Bhima Shankar Temple

Bhima Shankar Temple, located in the Sahyadri Hills, is another Jyotirlinga shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Siddhivinayak Temple

One of Mumbai's most famous landmarks, this temple is dedicated to Lord Ganesha and visited by thousands daily.

Grishneshwar Temple

Grishneshwar Temple near Ellora is another revered Jyotirlinga shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Mahalakshmi Temple

This historic temple in Kolhapur is dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi and is a part of the Shakti Peethas.

Ganpatipule Temple

Famous Temples in Kashmir

Explore the spiritual heritage of Kashmir through its famous temples.

Shankaracharya Temple

Located on Shankaracharya Hill, this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and offers a panoramic view of Srinagar and Dal Lake.

Martand Sun Temple

The Martand Sun Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to Surya, built by Lalitaditya Muktapida in the 8th century.

Kheer Bhawani Temple

Kheer Bhawani is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to the Goddess Bhawani, located near the village of Tulmul, 14 miles east of Srinagar.

Avantipur Temple

The Avantipur Temple complex, built by King Avantivarman in the 9th century, is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and exhibits remarkable stone carvings.

Devi Temple

Sharika Devi Temple is dedicated to Goddess Shakti, located on Hari Parbat Hill. The deity here is revered as an incarnation of the Mother Goddess.

Mamal Temple

Mamal Temple is a small Shiva temple located in Pahalgam, believed to have been built in the 8th century, with a charming setting near the Lidder River.

Pandrethan Temple

Pandrethan Temple, located near Srinagar, is a small ancient Hindu temple believed to have been constructed in the 9th century and dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Vaishno Devi Temple

Located in the Trikuta Mountains, Vaishno Devi is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi. Thousands of devotees visit this temple annually.

Adventures in Kashmir

Experience thrilling adventures in the scenic landscapes of Kashmir.

Skiing in Gulmarg

Gulmarg is famous for its world-class skiing and snowboarding slopes, offering an exhilarating experience amidst snow-covered peaks.

Trekking in Pahalgam

Explore breathtaking trails in Pahalgam, with treks that take you through stunning valleys, rivers, and alpine meadows.

River Rafting in Sonmarg

Experience thrilling river rafting in the fast-flowing rivers of Sonmarg, surrounded by lush green landscapes.

Fishing in Lidder River

Enjoy trout fishing in the serene Lidder River, a perfect retreat for those who love the tranquility of nature.

Camping in Aru Valley

Camp under the starry skies in Aru Valley, a picturesque spot with vast meadows and breathtaking mountain views.

Paragliding in Srinagar

Take to the skies with paragliding adventures in Srinagar, offering stunning aerial views of the city's lakes and landscapes.

Mountain Biking in Kashmir Valley

Embark on thrilling mountain biking trails across Kashmir Valley, navigating rugged terrains and scenic paths.

Hot Air Ballooning in Sonmarg

Experience a serene hot air balloon ride in Sonmarg, with panoramic views of lush valleys and the Himalayas.

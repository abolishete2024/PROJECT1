Famous Places in Kashmir

Discover the beauty and culture of Kashmir’s most famous landmarks.

Dal Lake

Known as the jewel of Srinagar, Dal Lake is famous for its shikara rides, houseboats, and beautiful surroundings.

Gulmarg

A popular skiing destination, Gulmarg is known for its scenic beauty and adventure sports.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam offers stunning landscapes, making it a popular spot for hiking, trekking, and relaxing by the river.

Sonmarg

Sonmarg, or the 'Meadow of Gold,' is known for its stunning valleys, rivers, and snow-capped peaks.

Betaab Valley

Famous for its lush green meadows and clear streams, Betaab Valley is a must-visit location in Kashmir.

Mughal Gardens

These historic gardens in Srinagar showcase Mughal architecture and beautiful floral layouts.

Wular Lake

One of the largest freshwater lakes in Asia, Wular Lake is a serene and picturesque spot for nature lovers.

Achabal Gardens

A charming Mughal garden with lush greenery, fountains, and beautiful scenery in Anantnag district.

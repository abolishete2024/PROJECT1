Famous Places in Maharashtra

Discover the beauty and heritage of Maharashtra's top landmarks and attractions.

Gateway of India

Located in Mumbai, this iconic monument overlooks the Arabian Sea.

Ajanta Caves

Explore ancient Buddhist caves featuring stunning rock-cut sculptures and paintings.

Mahabaleshwar

Known for its scenic viewpoints, lush greenery, and serene atmosphere.

Ellora Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, these caves showcase Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain temples.

Marine Drive

Known as the "Queen's Necklace," this iconic stretch offers mesmerizing sunset views.

Raigad Fort

A historic fort and former capital of the Maratha Empire, offering panoramic views.

Tadoba

The Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is a wildlife sanctuary in Chandrapur district of Maharashtra state in India.

Alibagh

Alibag, also known as Alibaug ,is a coastal city and a municipal council in Raigad district of Maharashtra, India.
